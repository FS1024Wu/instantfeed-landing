## 5. Notification Preferences & Opt-In/Opt-Out

### 5.1 Your Control

You have full control over communication preferences:

**Email Notifications:**
- You can unsubscribe from non-critical emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email
- Critical notifications (order confirmations, account security) cannot be disabled
- Update preferences in your account settings anytime

**SMS Notifications:**
- You can opt-out by replying "STOP" to any SMS message
- Update preferences in your account settings
- OTP (One-Time Password) messages for authentication are necessary for account access

**Push Notifications:**
- Disable push notifications in your device settings
- Manage notification preferences within MYFEED/MYSHOP app settings
- You can disable notifications for each category separately

### 5.2 How We Use These Channels

- **Email & SMS:** Order updates, account alerts, promotional offers, OTP verification
- **Push Notifications:** Real-time order status, new items, store updates, special offers
- **In-App Messages:** Service announcements, promotions, important updates

All notifications respect "Do Not Disturb" hours you may set in your device settings.

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication tokens
- Regular security audits and assessments
- Restricted access to personal data (need-to-know basis)
- Secure deletion of data when no longer needed

### 6.2 Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active; deleted upon account deletion (with 30-day grace period)
- **Transaction Data:** Retained for compliance and audit purposes (typically 7 years)
- **Communication Logs:** Retained for 12 months or as required by law
- **Device/Usage Analytics:** Retained for 90 days, then anonymized

### 6.3 Limitation of Liability

While we implement strong security measures, no internet transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your personal information. You use our Services at your own risk.
